# Competitive Positioning

## Target market

Vietnamese SMEs (10-200 employees) needing an all-in-one ERP solution.

## Competitors

| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | Our advantage |
|------------|-----------|------------|---------------|
| **Odoo** | Modular, mature, large community | Complex setup, expensive cloud, heavy | Lighter, faster, Vietnamese-first |
| **KiotViet** | Retail POS, Vietnamese-built | Limited features outside retail, closed source | Open source, full ERP, extensible |
| **Nhanh.vn** | Ecommerce + warehouse integration | Weak manufacturing/HR/accounting | Full suite, offline-first |
| **MISA** | Accounting, Vietnamese compliance | Legacy tech, not mobile-friendly | Modern tech stack, PWA, real-time |
| **ERPNext** | Open source, comprehensive | UI dated, complex customization | Modern UI, TypeScript, better UX |

## Key differentiators

1. **Offline-first**: Works without internet (IndexedDB + sync engine)
2. **Modern stack**: Next.js 15 + NestJS + TypeScript throughout
3. **Open core**: MIT license, self-hostable
4. **Vietnamese compliance**: VAT, e-invoice, accounting standards built-in
5. **AI-powered**: Demand forecasting, churn prediction, CLV analytics
6. **PWA**: Installable on mobile, works on slow networks

## Pricing strategy

- **Open source (free)**: Self-hosted, no license fee
- **Cloud (paid)**: Hosted SaaS with tiered pricing based on users/modules
- **Enterprise (custom)**: On-premise, custom modules, dedicated support
